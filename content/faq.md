## What is your hourly rate?
We don't charge hourly for standard builds. You pay upfront for the predefined scope so there are no surprises!

## How long does a website take?
A standard Custom 5-page site typically takes 2-4 weeks from discovery to final deployment. 

## Do I own my website?
Absolutely. We structure our contracts so that once the site is paid for, all code, assets, and design files are 100% yours.

## Do you offer hosting?
Yes! Our Monthly and Up-Front plans encompass basic hosting under the monthly maintenance fee to ensure your site is always online and up to date.
